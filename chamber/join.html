Become a Member of Porto Chamber of Commerce

Membership Benefits

Non Profit

Cost: No fees.

Benefits:
  • Regular updates and newsletters.
  • Networking opportunities.

Bronze Membership

Cost: Basic membership fee.

Benefits:
  • Access to newsletters.
  • Invitation to meetings.
  • Event volunteer opportunities.
  • Member discounts.
  • Recognition as Bronze member.

Silver Membership

Cost: Moderate membership fee.

Benefits:
  • All Bronze benefits.
  • Exclusive webinars/workshops.
  • Event discounts.
  • Silver member badge.
  • Inclusion in annual report.

Gold Membership

Cost: Highest membership fee.

Benefits:
  • All Silver benefits.
  • Access to networking events.
  • Premium advertising space.
  • Significant event discounts.
  • Gold member plaque.
  • Personalized recognition.
