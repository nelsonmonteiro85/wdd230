President's Message

As President of Porto Chamber of Commerce (PortoCC), it is a pleasure to welcome all those who visit us.

With this portal we seek to give you an idea of an institution that already counts 190 years of existence, without ever abandoning the purpose of its creation: to be a meeting point and a reflection center with the intention of promoting the progress and the illustration of the city of Porto and the Northern Region. This has been the motto of PortoCC throughout its existence and it is certainly the guiding line that will lead us in the future.
