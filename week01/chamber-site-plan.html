Purpose

Through this portal, we aim to showcase an institution with a rich 190-year history, dedicated unwaveringly to its founding mission: serving as a hub for dialogue and intellectual exploration, fostering the advancement and enlightenment of Porto and the Northern Region. Associação Comercial do Porto has steadfastly upheld this ethos throughout its existence, and it remains our guiding principle as we chart our course for the future.

Audience

We aim to serve a wide spectrum of stakeholders including local businesses, entrepreneurs, government entities, community members, and visitors, fostering economic growth, advocacy, and community development within its region.

Scenarios

What upcoming networking events are scheduled, and how can I register to attend? I'm looking to expand my professional network within the local business community.

Where can I find resources or guides on starting a business in Porto? I'm interested in understanding local regulations, permits, and available support services.

What are the latest economic trends or statistics for Porto? I'm researching potential locations for a new business venture and need insights into market growth and demographics.

How can I become a member of the Chamber of Commerce, and what are the benefits of membership?

Is there a directory of local businesses available on the Chamber of Commerce website? I'm looking for specific services or suppliers within Porto.
